Quick Facts

  1. Short for "bubble tea"
  2. A tea-based drink
  3. Started in the 1980s of Taiwan
  4. Distinctively includes chewy tapioca balls (the "boba" or "pearls")
  5. May or may not include milk

Teas

Boba tea can be used with different kind of tea, such as

  • Black Tea
  • Green Tea
  • Oolong Tea
  • White Tea

Flavors

Boba tea can also have different flavors, and there are many that people choose from. Flavored simple syrups (one way to flavor boba) can include:

  1. Strawberry
  2. Mango
  3. Plum
  4. Honeydew
  5. Pinapple
